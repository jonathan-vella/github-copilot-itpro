# Customer Scenario: Enterprise IT Operations Automation

## Company Profile

**Organization**: Global Manufacturing Corporation  
**Industry**: Manufacturing & Distribution  
**Size**: 5,000 employees, 15 countries  
**IT Team**: 12 Azure administrators  
**Azure Footprint**: 200+ subscriptions

## Business Challenge

**Current Pain Points:**

- Manual reporting takes 2 hours daily
- Inconsistent resource tagging
- $5,000/month wasted on orphaned resources
- 4 hours/week spent on compliance auditing

**Automation Goals:**

- Automated daily reports (5 min)
- Find untagged resources (10 min/week)
- Cleanup orphaned resources (15 min/week)
- Bulk tag operations (5-10 min)

## Expected ROI

**Time Savings**: 18 hours/week per admin  
**Cost Savings**: $63K/year in orphaned resources  
**Team Savings**: $842K/year (12 admins)  
**Azure Cost Savings**: $244K/year  
**Total Annual Value**: $1.086M
